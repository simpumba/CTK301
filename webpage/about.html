I’ve always had a deep connection with music, having even dabbled in piano for a brief time, but my true passion lies in sound design, especially in the world of video games. What fascinates me most is how sound can shape the entire gaming experience, from the emotional weight of a soundtrack to the impact of sound effects in combat, and even the subtle sounds that create a cozy atmosphere or ramp up the tension in a horror game. While I’ve only been honing my sound design skills for a few months, my fascination with its role in games has been growing for over a year. Watching video essays and learning how sound enhances the gaming experience led me to pursue sound design further and even start college for it. My work so far has focused on sound for animation, as it allows me to refine my skills and develop a solid foundation in sound fundamentals. I’ve also been experimenting with Unity projects to implement sound and gain hands-on experience. My approach to sound design is all about complementing the story and environment. Whether it’s creating an unsettling atmosphere for a tense moment or crafting sound effects that enhance combat, I aim to use sound to amplify the emotional depth of the narrative. Every element—from the background ambiance to the UI sounds—should help immerse the player in a believable, dynamic world. I am passionate about the potential of sound to transform a game into a fully realized experience. My goal is to focus on sound design for video games full-time, ultimately working with a team that shares my vision. One of my ultimate dreams is to contribute to the incredible work being done at Larian Studios.
